Project Overview: Strategic Customer Segmentation & Credit Risk Assessment

In the modern banking landscape, understanding customer behavior and mitigating financial risk are paramount. This project delivers a data-driven approach to Customer Segmentation and Credit Risk Analysis, leveraging advanced Machine Learning techniques to drive informed decision-making.


    Risk Mitigation: Developed a robust classification model to predict potential loan defaults, utilizing SMOTE to handle class imbalance effectively.

    Customer Insights: Applied clustering algorithms to segment the customer base, enabling personalized marketing strategies and improved resource allocation.

    Data-Driven Logic: Translated complex datasets into actionable business intelligence through rigorous Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and feature engineering.
